M I L E S T O N E S

FORMATION

Kerala Blasters FC was formed in 2014 as one of the inaugural teams in the Indian Super League

RUNNERS-UP IN INAUGURAL SEASON

Kerala Blasters reached the final in the inaugural season of the ISL in 2014 but finished as runners-up, losing to Atletico de Kolkata in the final.

ISL TITLE

In the 2016 season, Kerala Blasters once again reached the final and this time emerged victorious, defeating Atletico de Kolkata in a penalty shootout to clinch their first ISL title.

PARTICIPATION IN AFC CUP

Kerala Blasters represented India in the AFC Cup in 2018, becoming the first ISL team to participate in the competition.
